How it all started...

In 2017, I, the Founder of PainLessPvP, made a Youtube account uploading gaming content.

In 2018, I created BestPvP, that's what it was called back then.

In 2019, I decided to stop uploading and return back later to upload videos related on coding and renamed it to PainLessPvP.

But I never got the chance to upload videos.

In 2020 3 days after Christmas my minecraft account got hacked. 2 months after my account got hacked, my friend gave me his account because he quit mc.

Before he gave me his account, I decided to make the official website of PainLessPvP which took me 1 month to make somewhat it decent.


Our Goal

Our goal is to make the world's lowest ping Minecraft server.

Our servers will include FPS optimizations for the best performance for Low End PCs to prevent people from lagging in any way or form.
